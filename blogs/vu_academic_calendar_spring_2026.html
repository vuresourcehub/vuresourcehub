Mid-Term & Final-Term Exams

The two most critical academic events of the Spring 2026 semester are the mid-term and final-term examinations. Planning your preparation around these dates is essential for academic success.

Mid-Term Exam – Spring 2026

The VU Mid-Term Exam for Spring 2026 is scheduled to begin on Monday, June 1, 2026. Students are advised to complete at least the first half of their course material well before this date. Mid-term exams typically cover the initial weeks of the semester and contribute significantly to the final grade.

Final-Term Exam – Spring 2026

The Final-Term Exam for Spring 2026 begins on Monday, July 27, 2026. This is the last major assessment of the semester. Students must ensure all assignments, GDBs, and quizzes are submitted before attempting the final exam to avoid any grade penalties.

Pro Tip: Start preparing for your mid-term at least 3 weeks in advance. For the final-term, begin revision after mid-term results are announced. Consistent preparation is far more effective than last-minute cramming.
